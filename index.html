Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about migrating to Medical365.

Is Medical365 compliant with Indian Government standards?
Yes, absolutely. Medical365 is fully compliant with the Ayushman Bharat Digital Mission (ABDM), supports ABHA ID generation, and meets all electronic health record standards defined by the MoHFW, India.
How long does it take to deploy in a multi-specialty hospital?
Our cloud-native architecture allows for incredibly fast deployment. While traditional systems take months, a standard mid-sized hospital can go live with Medical365 in just 2 to 4 weeks, including staff training and initial data migration.
Can Medical365 integrate with our existing lab machines (LIMS)?
Yes. We provide bidirectional integration with most modern lab analyzers and radiology equipment (PACS/DICOM). This ensures test results are automatically synced directly into the patient's EMR, eliminating manual data entry.
Is our hospital data secure in the cloud?
Security is our top priority. Your data is protected by enterprise-grade encryption (both at rest and in transit). The platform is ready for the DPDP Act 2023 and ensures strict role-based access for your staff.
Do you provide support for Multi-chain Clinics?
Yes, Medical365 has a dedicated "Multi-Facility Operations" module. It allows management to view consolidated financial and clinical reports across all branches from a single dashboard, while keeping data securely partitioned per location.
