Pricing FAQs

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time. The difference in pricing will be calculated pro-rata for the remaining period.

Are there any setup fees?

We provide free online onboarding. For on-site deployment and training in specific locations, a one-time implementation fee may apply based on the facility size.

Is my data safe if I cancel?

Absolutely. You own your data. If you decide to leave, we provide a full data export in standard formats (CSV/Excel) as per our data portability policy.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit/debit cards, Net Banking, and UPI payments through our secure payment gateway.
