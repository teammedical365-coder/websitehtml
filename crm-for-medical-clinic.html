People Also Search (FAQ)

Get instant answers to the most common questions regarding medical clinic CRM selection, compliance, and integration.

Why does a medical clinic need a specialized CRM instead of a generic one?
Generic CRMs like Salesforce or HubSpot are built for traditional sales pipelines and do not comply with healthcare data privacy laws (like HIPAA or the Indian DPDP Act 2023) out-of-the-box. Medical365 is a specialized healthcare CRM that safeguards patient health records (EHRs), features native EMR integrations, and automates patient recall processes without violating privacy standards.
Is Medical365 Clinic CRM HIPAA and DPDP Act 2023 compliant?
Yes, Medical365 is 100% compliant with the DPDP Act 2023 (Digital Personal Data Protection Act of India) and HIPAA guidelines. We store all healthcare database assets in highly secure, encrypted data vaults located locally within Indian borders, ensuring zero data breach risk and legal compliance.
How does EMR/EHR integration work with Medical365 CRM?
Medical365 CRM is natively integrated with our industry-leading EMR/EHR system. This allows the software to automatically synchronize appointment dates, diagnostic reports, and outpatient (OPD) histories with the patient lifecycle manager, creating a single unified profile for every patient.
Can we automate patient recall campaigns and appointment reminders?
Absolutely. You can schedule automated recall campaigns for chronic health checkups, follow-ups, pediatric vaccinations, and annual assessments. The system triggers these alerts automatically via WhatsApp Business API or SMS according to the criteria defined by your clinic.
Does Medical365 CRM support WhatsApp Business API integrations?
Yes. Medical365 provides native integration with the WhatsApp Business API. Clinics can send automated prescription summaries, digital token queue updates, review requests, and appointment booking confirmations directly to the patient's WhatsApp window, achieving 3x higher response rates than email.
