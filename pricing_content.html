Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Medical365 billing and plans.

What does "All Facilities Included" mean?

Every plan comes with full access to our core modules including Patient Registration, EMR/EHR, Billing & Invoicing, WhatsApp Notifications, and Data Security. We don't limit features by tier, only user limits.

Can I upgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can upgrade your plan at any time to add more doctors, staff accounts, or branch locations. The prorated amount will simply be applied to your new subscription cycle.

Is there a setup fee?

There are no hidden setup fees. The annual subscription covers onboarding, basic training, and support. Custom localized modules or API development may require a separate quote.
