Our Journey

From a small team with a big idea to the platform trusted by 500+ institutions.

2012

Founded in Bangalore

Medical365 started as a clinic management tool built for a single 3-doctor practice. The founders immediately recognized the depth of the problem.

2015

First 50 Hospital Clients

After years of listening to clinicians, we launched our hospital module and reached 50 institutional clients across South India.

2018

Cloud-Native Platform Launch

Rebuilt the entire platform from the ground up as a cloud-native solution with real-time analytics and mobile-first workflows.

2021

Telemedicine & Patient Portal

Launched integrated telemedicine and the patient-facing portal in response to the telehealth surge. 100,000 virtual consultations in year one.

2024

500+ Institutions & Expanding

Today Medical365 serves over 500 healthcare institutions, 2 million patients, and continues to expand across South Asia and the Middle East.
