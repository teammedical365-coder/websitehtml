Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to the questions we hear most often from healthcare providers.

How long does implementation take? +

Most clinics go live within 3–5 days. Multi-specialty hospitals typically take 2–4 weeks depending on scope. We handle the entire setup and data migration.

Is Medical365 government compliant? +

Yes, fully. Medical365 is completely ABDM compliant, ABHA integrated, and ready for the DPDP Act 2023 with end-to-end encryption and audit trails for all data access.

Can it integrate with existing lab or imaging systems? +

Yes. Medical365 integrates with all major LIS, RIS, and PACS systems via HL7 and FHIR-compliant APIs. Our team handles the integration setup.

What kind of support do you offer? +

We offer 24/7 technical support, dedicated onboarding, regular training sessions, and a named account manager for all enterprise clients.

Can I migrate data from my existing software? +

Absolutely. Our data migration team has successfully migrated from over 40 different legacy systems. We ensure zero data loss and minimal downtime.

Is there a free trial available? +

We offer a free personalized demo and a 30-day pilot program for qualifying facilities, so you can experience Medical365 in your real environment before committing.
